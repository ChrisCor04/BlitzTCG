What is BlitzTCG?


BlitzTCG is a comprehensive tool for tracking and managing your trading card collection. Monitor real-time price changes and market trends with ease. We use reliable APIs to fetch up-to-date card pricing data and provide you with market movements. This passion project is built to help collectors save time and track their investments and collections with ease. If you have any suggestions or feedback, feel free to reach out at itzblitztcg@gmail.com!

How do I use BlitzTCG?


Simply sign up, add your cards to your collection, and start tracking their value over time. Sometimes the card you want to add may not appear right away, if this occurs try searching up the card with the exact code following it for example you may search "Greninja ex" and not see a specific version of the card, in this case try searching "Greninja ex - 214/167" to find the exact card you want to add. You can view historical price trends of your collection through our charts and analyze market data to make informed decisions about buying or selling cards.
